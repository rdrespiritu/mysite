Born in the heart of Manila, Philippines, Rafael has always been considered different from the rest of the kids of his age. As a kid, he wanted to be an Architect, but fate had a different plan for him. After spending some time in college, he decided to drop out due to some issues with his college scholarship. He started working as a food service crew member and eventually landed a job as a call center representative.

Fueled by his desire to alleviate his career and search for financial freedom, he decided to take a leap of faith and entered the world of Freelancing. Armed with an old second-hand laptop, a strong determination to succeed, and a thirst for knowledge, he embarked on a new quest on this uncertain path. His first breakthrough came when he secured his first freelance contract for a VOIP provider in Canada as a project manager and customer support trainer.

His stint with the company led to referrals that laid the foundation for his thriving freelance career. He honed the skills he gained from his call center career and used them to his full advantage and relied on these experiences to land jobs as a customer cupport representative and team manager. He also used this opportunity to further enhance his knowledge by teaching himself new skills like graphic and logo design.

His newly acquired skills and knowledge led to the opening of more opportunities for him. As a way of challenging himself, he took part in some Logo design contests and won several of them. The word of his talent spread quickly and led him to land graphic design projects for start-up companies from various parts of the world. His experiences gave him the opportunity to secure a visa to study in Canada as an international itudent and hopefully establish a career in the creative design industry.

Today, he is in his first term as a Multimedia Production student at Lethbridge College, Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada. His journey from a predictable 9-5 routine to a prosperous career as a freelancer serves as an inspiration to several individuals seeking freedom in their professional lives. With his determination and adaptability, Rafael is a reminder that in the world of freelancing, success is only limited by how much you are willing to sacrifice, and success is attainable for those who are willing to take the risk and bold enough to take the leap of faith to achieve their goals.
